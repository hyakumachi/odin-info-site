Who is Daigo?

Born in 1981 in Okayama, Japan, Daigo Umehara is widely regarded as the greatest Street Fighter player of all time. He turned professional in 2010, becoming one of the first esports athletes in Japan.

Career

1998

EVO debut

Won Street Fighter Alpha 3 at EVO, first putting his name on the international map.

2003–05

3rd Strike dominance

Won EVO's Street Fighter III: 3rd Strike three consecutive years, cementing his reputation.

2004

EVO Moment #37

In a semifinal against Justin Wong, Daigo parried a full Chun-Li super combo with one pixel of life left — now considered the most iconic moment in fighting game history.

2010

Goes pro

Signed by Mad Catz, becoming one of Japan's first professional esports players.

2026

Still competing

Continues to compete at the highest level in Street Fighter and other titles, streaming and inspiring a new generation.

Beyond the game

Daigo authored The Will to Keep Winning, a book on mindset, dedication, and what it takes to stay at the top. It reflects his philosophy: improvement over results, always.
